Book Library

The Great Gatsby

The American Dream

Author: F. Scott Fitzgerald

Publisher: Scribner

Published: April 10, 1925

Set in the Jazz Age on Long Island, the novel depicts narrator Nick Carraway's interactions with mysterious millionaire Jay Gatsby and Gatsby's obsession to reunite with his former lover, Daisy Buchanan.

To Kill a Mockingbird

A Story of Justice and Innocence

Author: Harper Lee

Publisher: J.B. Lippincott & Co.

Published: July 11, 1960

The unforgettable novel of a childhood in a sleepy Southern town and the crisis of conscience that rocked it. It's the story of Atticus Finch, a lawyer who defends a Black man accused of a terrible crime.

1984

A Dystopian Social Science Fiction

Author: George Orwell

Publisher: Secker & Warburg

Published: June 8, 1949

The novel is set in Airstrip One, a province of the superstate Oceania in a world of perpetual war, omnipresent government surveillance, and public manipulation.

Pride and Prejudice

A Romantic Novel

Author: Jane Austen

Publisher: T. Egerton

Published: January 28, 1813

The story follows the main character, Elizabeth Bennet, as she deals with issues of manners, upbringing, morality, education, and marriage in the society of the landed gentry of the British Regency.

The Hobbit

Or There and Back Again

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Publisher: George Allen & Unwin

Published: September 21, 1937

The story is about the hobbit Bilbo Baggins, who is hired by the wizard Gandalf to accompany 13 dwarves led by Thorin Oakenshield on a quest to reclaim the Lonely Mountain from the dragon Smaug.

The Catcher in the Rye

A Novel by J.D. Salinger

Author: J.D. Salinger

Publisher: Little, Brown and Company

Published: July 16, 1951

The novel details two days in the life of 16-year-old Holden Caulfield after he has been expelled from prep school. Confused and disillusioned, he searches for truth and rails against the "phoniness" of the adult world.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

The Boy Who Lived

Author: J.K. Rowling

Publisher: Bloomsbury

Published: June 26, 1997

The first novel in the Harry Potter series follows Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday, when he receives a letter of acceptance to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Lord of the Rings

The Fellowship of the Ring

Author: J.R.R. Tolkien

Publisher: Allen & Unwin

Published: July 29, 1954

The first volume in the trilogy follows the hobbit Frodo Baggins as he and the Fellowship embark on a quest to destroy the One Ring, to ensure the destruction of its maker, the Dark Lord Sauron.
